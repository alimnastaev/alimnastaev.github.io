Alim was born in Nalchik, Russia, where he developed his love for music at a very early age, resulting in winning and playing many international competitions along with major festivals in Europe (Montreux Jazz Festival 2006, Usadba Jazz 2007).

In 2009 Alim moved to the US to pursue his master’s degree at The Jamey Aeborsold Jazz Program (University of Louisville), supported by a GTA full scholarship.

He recorded, co-wrote and produced two full length CD’s with the group AXIS and Alim Nastaev Band, touring extensively throughout Russia, Europe, and the USA.
